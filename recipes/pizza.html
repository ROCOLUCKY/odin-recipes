Pizza Recipe: Dough and Sauce

Ingredients for Pizza Dough:

  • 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast (1 packet)
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 cup warm water (about 110°F)
  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Ingredients for Pizza Sauce:

  • 1 can (15 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon sugar (optional, to taste)

Instructions for Pizza Dough:

  1. In a small bowl, dissolve yeast and sugar in warm water. Let sit until frothy, about 10 minutes.
  2. In a large bowl, combine flour, salt, olive oil, and the yeast mixture. Stir until the dough comes together.
  3. Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and knead until smooth and elastic, about 7-10 minutes.
  4. Place the dough in a well-oiled bowl, cover with a damp cloth, and let rise in a warm place until doubled in size, about 1 hour.
  5. Once risen, punch down the dough and form it into a ball. Let it rest for a few minutes before stretching it out into a pizza shape.

Instructions for Pizza Sauce:

  1. Heat olive oil in a saucepan over medium heat. Add minced garlic and sauté until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  2. Add crushed tomatoes, oregano, basil, salt, pepper, and sugar if using. Simmer for about 20-30 minutes until the sauce thickens slightly.
  3. Adjust seasoning to taste.

Assembly and Baking:

  1. Preheat your oven to the highest setting, typically between 450°F to 500°F (230°C to 260°C).
  2. Stretch the dough on a pizza stone or a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  3. Spread the sauce over the dough, add your choice of toppings, and sprinkle with cheese.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven for about 10-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  5. Remove from oven, let cool slightly, and serve.
