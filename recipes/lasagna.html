Lasagne al Forno

Ingredients:

For the Bolognese Sauce:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 carrot, finely chopped
  • 1 celery stalk, finely chopped
  • 500g ground beef
  • 800g canned crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 cup red wine (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano or mixed herbs
  • A pinch of sugar (optional)

For the Béchamel Sauce:

  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 4 cups milk
  • A pinch of nutmeg
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Additional Ingredients:

  • Lasagne noodles (pre-cooked or no-boil)
  • 2 cups grated mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions:

1. Prepare the Bolognese Sauce:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add the onion, garlic, carrot, and celery. Cook until softened.
  2. Increase the heat, add the ground beef and cook until browned. Break up any large pieces with a spoon.
  3. Add the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, red wine (if using), salt, pepper, herbs, and a pinch of sugar. Simmer on low heat for about 30 minutes or until thickened. Stir occasionally.

2. Prepare the Béchamel Sauce:

  1. Melt the butter in a saucepan over medium heat. Add the flour and cook, stirring, for 1-2 minutes without letting it brown.
  2. Gradually whisk in the milk to avoid lumps. Cook, stirring continuously, until the sauce thickens. Season with nutmeg, salt, and pepper. Remove from heat.

3. Assemble the Lasagne:

  1. Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Spread a thin layer of Bolognese sauce on the bottom of a baking dish. Place a layer of lasagne noodles over the sauce.
  3. Spread a layer of Bolognese sauce over the noodles, followed by a layer of Béchamel sauce. Sprinkle with mozzarella.
  4. Repeat the layers (noodles, Bolognese, Béchamel, mozzarella) until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of Béchamel sauce. Sprinkle the top with grated Parmesan cheese.
  5. Cover with foil and bake for 20 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 20 minutes or until the top is golden and bubbling.

4. Serving:

  1. Let the lasagne sit for about 10 minutes after baking. This makes it easier to cut and serve.
  2. Serve hot with a side salad or bread, if desired.
